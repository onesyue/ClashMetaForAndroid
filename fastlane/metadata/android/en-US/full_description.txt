YueTong — Global acceleration network for AI and streaming.

<h3>Features</h3>
<ul>
<li>Native login and registration with XBoard panel integration</li>
<li>Auto-sync subscriptions with support for hysteria2, vless, and reality protocols</li>
<li>Real-time VPN dashboard: upload/download speed, traffic usage, connection duration</li>
<li>Account center: plan details, expiry, balance, referral link</li>
<li>In-app store with coupon support and multiple billing periods</li>
<li>Order history with status tracking and cancellation</li>
<li>Panel announcements with Markdown rendering</li>
<li>Built-in DNS server with DoH/DoT support and fake IP</li>
<li>Rules based on domains, GEOIP, IPCIDR, or process name</li>
<li>Remote groups with automatic fallback, load balancing, and latency-based selection</li>
</ul>
